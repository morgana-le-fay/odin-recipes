Norse Fish Soup

How To Make Fish Soup

Try this traditional Norwegian fish soup on a cold autumn day. Choose your favorite fish, either haddock, cod or wolffish, they are all suitable in the soup.

Ingredients

  • haddock fillet
  • caroot
  • butter
  • fish stock
  • lemon
  • double cream
  • curry
  • chives, fresh

Steps

  1. Dice the fish, and carrot, celeriac and root parsley.
  2. Melt butter and mix in flour and fish stock until thicc
  3. Add veggies and let boil about 15 mins, then removing veggies from soup.
  4. Ad remianing ingredients to soup, let cook for five minutes until fish is done.
